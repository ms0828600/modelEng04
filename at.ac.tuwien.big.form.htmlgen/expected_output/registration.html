Registration conditions

To be able to register, you have to fulfill the following conditions:

  • You have to live in Austria.
  • You have to be current full-time student of a Viennese university.

Registration procedure

The registration is a two-phase process:

  1. First you have to fill in the registration form.
  2. Your application will be confirmed by E-Mail.
